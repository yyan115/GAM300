About the Game

A 3D action platformer featuring chain-based combat mechanics, feather skill abilities, and a variety of enemy types across multiple levels. Built from scratch with a custom ECS game engine supporting Windows and Android.

Chain Combat

Chain attacks, dash, dodge, and grapple your way through enemies and bosses.

Feather Skills

Collect feathers to unlock and use special abilities throughout the game.

Cross-Platform

Play on Windows or Android, built on a custom engine with platform-specific optimizations.

Custom Engine

ECS architecture with Lua scripting, Jolt physics, and OpenGL/ES rendering.
