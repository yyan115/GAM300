Kusane

A dark action platformer built on chains, blades, and unforgiving combat.

The Game

Fight with chains. Kill with precision.

Kusane is a 3D action platformer where you wield a chain weapon alongside melee slashes to tear through waves of ground and airborne enemies. Hook targets from a distance, pull them in, and finish them with devastating combo strings. Every encounter demands sharp reflexes and smart positioning.

Collect feathers from fallen enemies to charge a powerful AoE blast that clears the field when you're overwhelmed. Time your dodges to trigger slow-motion windows that let you punish overcommitted enemies.

Combat

Chain attacks, airborne combos, and a boss that evolves.

The chain isn't just a weapon — it's how you move, how you interact, and how you survive. Grapple to points across the level, rip open chain-locked doors while fending off enemies, and aim manually to snag exactly the target you want.

Aerial Combat

Launch enemies into the air and follow up with mid-air slash combos and jump extensions. Chain between ground and air seamlessly.

Multi-Phase Boss

Face a miniboss whose abilities evolve across phases — from dash attacks to feather bombs to rapid projectile bursts. Adapt or die.

Presentation

Voice-acted cutscenes, hand-crafted art, and cinematic effects.

Opening and ending cutscenes with full voice-over set the tone for a world that takes itself seriously. In-game, screen shake, motion blur, chromatic aberration, and distance-based camera effects make every hit feel impactful.

Visual Polish

Post-processing effects layer on vignette, chromatic aberration, and motion blur to create a dark, cinematic atmosphere throughout gameplay.

Custom Engine

Built from scratch in C++ with an ECS architecture, Jolt physics, Lua scripting, and OpenGL rendering. Runs natively on both Windows and Android.
